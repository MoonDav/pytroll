=============
Workshop 2012
=============

The first Pytroll workshop held in Norrk√∂ping, Sweden, end of November, 2012,
has come to an end. 9 people from Holland, Finland, Romania and EUMETSAT
joined up with the pytroll teams at DMI and SMHI, to get more acquainted
with the pytroll tools and how it can be used in their local environments
for satellite data production.
